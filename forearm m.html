Forearm Exercises

Wrist Curls (Barbell)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Sit on a bench, hold a barbell with palms up, rest forearms on thighs, curl the barbell using only your wrists, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens forearm flexors, improves grip strength and wrist stability.

Reverse Wrist Curls (Barbell)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a barbell with palms facing down, rest forearms on thighs, lift the bar using only your wrists, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens forearm extensors, improves wrist balance and grip endurance.

Hammer Curls

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold dumbbells with palms facing each other, curl the weights up while keeping elbows close to torso, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Builds brachioradialis and forearm muscles, enhances grip strength and arm thickness.

Reversed Dumbbell Curl

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Stand upright holding dumbbells with palms facing down, curl the weights toward your shoulders while keeping elbows close, then slowly lower.

Benefits: Strengthens forearms and brachioradialis, improves grip and arm definition.
