Quadriceps Exercises

Squats (Barbell)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body by bending knees and hips, then return upright.

Benefits: Builds quadriceps, glutes, core stability, and overall lower body strength.

Leg Press

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Sit on leg press machine, push the platform with feet, then slowly return.

Benefits: Strengthens quadriceps, reduces spine load compared to squats, good for hypertrophy.

Lunges

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Step forward with one leg, lower hips until both knees are bent 90°, push back to start, repeat other leg.

Benefits: Builds quads, glutes, improves balance and unilateral strength.

Bulgarian Split Squats

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Place back foot on a bench, lower front knee to 90°, push back up.

Benefits: Isolates quads, improves balance, strengthens legs unilaterally.

Leg Extensions

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Sit on leg extension machine, extend knees to lift weight, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Isolates quadriceps, great for hypertrophy and muscle definition.
