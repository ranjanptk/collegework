Hammer Curls

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold dumbbells with your palms facing each other (neutral grip). Curl the weights up while keeping elbows close to your body, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens forearms, brachioradialis, and improves grip strength.
