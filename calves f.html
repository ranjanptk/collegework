Calves Exercises

Standing Calf Raise

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Stand on a raised platform or step with your heels hanging off. Push through the balls of your feet to lift your body, then slowly lower back down.

Benefits: Strengthens gastrocnemius and soleus muscles, improves ankle stability, enhances jump power and balance.

Seated Calf Raise

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Sit on a bench with feet on the platform, place weight on your knees. Raise your heels as high as possible, pause, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Targets soleus muscle, improves lower leg endurance, enhances posture and walking efficiency.

Jump Rope Calf Raises

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a jump rope and jump continuously on your toes. Focus on pushing through the balls of your feet with each jump, keeping your knees slightly bent.

Benefits: Strengthens gastrocnemius and soleus, enhances calf endurance, improves coordination, and boosts cardiovascular fitness.

Smith Machine Calf Raise

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Position yourself under the Smith machine bar with the balls of your feet on a raised platform. Press through your toes to lift the bar, pause at the top, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Provides heavy-load calf strengthening, improves muscle definition, and allows controlled movement for safety and progressive overload.

Box Jump Calf Push

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Stand in front of a low box or platform. Jump onto it using primarily calf power, land softly, and step back down. Repeat for reps.

Benefits: Develops explosive calf power, strengthens lower leg muscles, and improves vertical jump height.
