Triceps Exercises

Tricep Dips

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Place hands on a bench or parallel bars, lower your body by bending elbows, then push back up using triceps.

Benefits: Strengthens triceps, chest, and shoulders, improves upper body pushing power.

Overhead Dumbbell Extension

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a dumbbell overhead with both hands, lower it behind your head by bending elbows, then extend back up.
Each side equal sets.

Benefits: Targets long head of triceps, improves arm size and strength.

Cable Tricep Pushdown

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Stand at a cable machine, grip bar or rope, push down by extending elbows fully, then slowly return.

Benefits: Isolates triceps, builds definition and strength, protects elbow joints.

Close-Grip Bench Press

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie on a bench, grip the barbell narrower than shoulder width, lower it to your chest, then push back up focusing on triceps.

Benefits: Builds overall triceps strength, also engages chest and shoulders, improves pressing power.

Kickbacks (Dumbbell)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a dumbbell in each hand, hinge at hips, keep elbows close to torso, extend forearm back until straight, then return.

Benefits: Isolates triceps, improves arm definition, enhances elbow stability.
