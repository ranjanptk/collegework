Trapezius Exercises

Barbell Shrugs

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a barbell in front of thighs, shrug shoulders straight up, pause, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Builds upper traps, improves posture and neck support, enhances overall shoulder aesthetics.

Dumbbell Shrugs

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold dumbbells at your sides, shrug shoulders upward, hold, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Isolates traps, increases shoulder width, and balances symmetry with other upper body muscles.

Face Pulls (Cable)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Attach rope to cable at face level, pull towards face while flaring elbows, then slowly release.

Benefits: Strengthens rear delts and traps, improves posture and shoulder stability.

Upright Rows (Barbell or Dumbbell)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold barbell/dumbbells in front, lift straight up to collarbone while keeping elbows high, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Builds traps and medial delts, improves shoulder definition and strength.

Barbell Behind-the-Back Shrugs

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a barbell behind your hips with an overhand grip. Keep your arms straight and shrug your shoulders straight up toward your ears. Hold briefly at the top, then slowly lower the barbell back down.

Benefits: Emphasizes middle and upper traps, improves shoulder stability and posture, and provides a different stimulus than front shrugs.
