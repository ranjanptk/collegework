Trapezius Exercises

Barbell Shrugs

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a barbell with both hands in front of your thighs. Shrug your shoulders upward as high as possible, pause, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Builds strong upper traps, increases shoulder stability, and allows heavy progressive overload.

Dumbbell Shrugs

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a dumbbell in each hand at your sides. Shrug shoulders upward, hold for a moment, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens traps unilaterally, improves shoulder symmetry, and isolates upper traps effectively.

Dumbbell Incline Shrugs

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie chest-down on an incline bench holding dumbbells in each hand. Shrug your shoulders upward as high as possible, pause, then slowly lower.

Benefits: Targets upper traps with a stretch, improves shoulder posture, and strengthens trap muscles safely.

Dumbbell Reverse Flys

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a dumbbell in each hand and bend forward at the hips with a straight back. Lift your arms out to the sides until parallel to the floor, then slowly lower.

Benefits: Strengthens middle and lower traps, rear delts, improves posture and upper back stability.

Face Pulls

How to do it: Use a cable with rope attachment at upper chest height. Pull rope towards face, elbows high, then slowly release.

Benefits: Strengthens traps and rear delts, improves posture and shoulder stability.
