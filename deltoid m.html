How to do it: Stand with feet shoulder-width, hold barbell at shoulder height, press overhead until arms are fully extended, then lower slowly.

How to do it: Stand holding dumbbells at your sides, lift arms out to shoulder height, then lower slowly.

Front Raises (Plate)

How to do it: Hold plate in front of thighs, lift them straight in front to shoulder height, then lower slowly.

How to do it: Bend forward at the hips holding dumbbells, lift arms out to sides, squeeze shoulder blades, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens posterior delts, improves posture, balances shoulder development.
