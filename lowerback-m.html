Lower Back Exercises

Hyperextensions (Back Extensions)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie on a hyperextension bench, lower torso slowly, then raise until body is straight.

Benefits: Strengthens erector spinae, improves posture, reduces risk of lower back injuries.

Deadlifts

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Stand with feet hip-width, grip barbell, lift with back and legs, keeping spine neutral, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens entire posterior chain including lower back, glutes, and hamstrings.

Romanian Deadlifts (RDL)

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Hold a barbell with feet hip-width apart, hinge at hips while keeping your back straight, lower the barbell down your legs, then return to standing.

Benefits: Strengthens lower back, hamstrings, and glutes, improves hip hinge mechanics, and enhances posterior chain stability.

Supermans

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie face down, lift arms and legs simultaneously, hold briefly, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens lower back and glutes, improves core stability and posture.

Bird Dogs

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: On hands and knees, extend opposite arm and leg simultaneously, hold, then switch sides.

Benefits: Strengthens lower back, core, and glutes, enhances balance and spinal stability.
