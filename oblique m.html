Oblique Exercises

Oblique V-Up

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie on your side, extend legs and torso, lift both legs and upper body simultaneously towards each other, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Targets obliques and side abs, improves core strength, enhances abdominal definition.

Incline Side Bend

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Sit on an incline bench sideways, bend your torso down then slowly return to starting position. Repeat for the other side.

Benefits: Strengthens obliques, improves lateral flexion, enhances side abdominal definition and core stability.

Cable Knee Crunch

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Kneel in front of a high pulley with rope attachment, grip the rope, bring your knees toward your chest while crunching your abs, then slowly return to starting position.

Benefits: Targets upper and lower abs, strengthens core, improves ab contraction and stability.

Bicycle Crunches

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie on your back, hands behind head, bring opposite elbow to opposite knee while extending the other leg, alternate sides.

Benefits: Activates obliques, improves abdominal endurance, strengthens core muscles.

Side Crunches

How to do it: Lie on your side, bend your knees slightly, lift your upper torso toward your hips, then lower slowly.
