Crunches

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie on your back with knees bent, hands behind your head. Lift your shoulders off the floor using your abs, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens upper abs, improves core endurance, and posture.

Plank

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Support your body on forearms and toes, keeping a straight line from head to heels. Hold without letting hips sag.

Benefits: Strengthens entire core, improves stability, posture, and endurance.

Bicycle Crunches

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie on your back, lift shoulders off floor, alternate touching opposite elbow to knee in a cycling motion.

Benefits: Targets obliques and rectus abdominis, improves rotational core strength.

Leg Raises

How to do it: Lie on your back, legs straight. Lift legs to vertical, then slowly lower without touching the floor.

Benefits: Strengthens lower abs, hip flexors, improves core stability.
