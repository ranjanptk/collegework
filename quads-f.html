Squats

How to do it: Stand with feet shoulder-width, lower hips down as if sitting, then rise back up.

Lunges

How to do it: Step forward with one leg, lower until both knees are at 90°, then push back up. Do equal set with both foot.

Benefits: Tones quads and glutes, improves stability and coordination.

Glute Bridge

Loading recommended sets/reps...

How to do it: Lie on your back with knees bent, feet flat on the floor. Lift your hips toward the ceiling, squeeze glutes at the top, then lower slowly.

Benefits: Strengthens glutes, hamstrings, and lower back; improves hip stability and posture.

How to do it: Step onto a bench with one leg, push through the heel, then step down carefully.

Benefits: Strengthens quads, glutes, and improves single-leg stability.
